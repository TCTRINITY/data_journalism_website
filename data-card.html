Exploring Cancer Death Rates About the Data

Here is the link to the dataset I used: https://corgis-edu.github.io/corgis/csv/cancer/

I used a CSV file from the CORGIS Dataset Project. This is a dataset with information regarding cancer death rates in each state from 2007-2013. In this dataset, there is information on cancer death rates (per 100,000 population) with regard to different factors such as race, age, and sex. In addition, there is information on death rates for three different types of cancer: breast, colorectal and lung. I decided to focus on three of the columns from this dataset which were "Types.Lung.Total," "Types.Breast.Total," and "Types.Colorectal.Total."
